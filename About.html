History of India & Culture — About

curated by Sudeep Bhowmik.

What this page is about

This site dives into the epic timeline of the Indian subcontinent and celebrates its living culture — from ancient civilisations and mighty empires to festivals, arts, and everyday traditions. We’re here for the history tea, the cultural aesthetics, and the stories that made modern India.

Our Mission

To present fascinating, reputable snapshots of India’s past and cultural heritage in a way that’s easy to read, visually pleasing, and respectful of diverse perspectives. We prioritize trusted sources and highlight artifacts, monuments, and traditions that shaped communities across time.

What you'll find here

History — timeline & highlights

Short, snackable histories: Indus Valley beginnings, Vedic-era developments, the Mauryan empire (hello Ashoka), the Mughal age, colonial encounters, and modern independence movements. Each page links to deeper reads and primary-site references.

Culture — living heritage

Festivals, music, dance, crafts, languages, and intangible heritage (like yoga, chanting, and regional rituals) — with notes on UNESCO-recognized traditions and world heritage places you can explore further.

Why trust us?

We base our summaries on established reference works and international heritage organizations, and we link to original articles so you can fact-check or dig deeper. If you want a deep dive on any era or cultural thread, hit the contact form and we’ll make a page.

  • Mid: Mauryan consolidation & Ashoka’s turn to dhamma.
